What's in store?

I don't have a strong order decided for building these - but this is what you can expect in the next 3 months, maybe sooner:

  • Social groups. You'll be able to add friends and have a dedicated leaderboard tab for the people in your social group.

  • Sharable log of your puzzle guesses. You know the green and yellow emojis Wordle uses? Yeah like that.

  • Past Puzzles. You won't get any bonuses from playing them, but you will get the base points.

  • A real domain name. This will also come with a paid server, so there's no waiting for a puzzle to load

  • A new game-mode for finding all possible answers of a puzzle. This will feature a sweet branching tree that will mark your progress

  • Weekly Challenge. A much longer puzzle. This will also be where I test out different possible rule-sets

  • Suggest a word. You'll highlight the spot in a puzzle and what word you think it's missing. It'll get sent to me to review and add to the puzzle while it's still up

  • Updating the points system. Instead of getting bonuses as you finish, you'll get them at the end of the day. That way you're ranked against everyone who finished - not just those who finished before you.

    Also, bronze medals.

  • Creating a visual theme for the puzzles. It'll give a unique feel rather than the slightly boring modern look.

After most of the above, I'll be adding some discrete ads. I have an awesome idea in mind for adding an entirely new system to play with, but it'll need to be a full blown game. With a budget.

What even is Scrambler?

Well, Scrambler is a little game that I came up with at a house party (I know, real rager). I drew the idea out on the back of a plastic plate and people actually like it!

It's been almost a year since then. I taught myself web-development to be able to build this (I code in Python otherwise) and share with all of you.

I've got HUGE ideas for things I'd like to do with this, but they all need players. Shout-out to my friends who've been my first playtesters. If you're enjoying the game, please share with your friends so I can build all the cool things I want to show you :)

HOW TO PLAY
Hey there!

Welcome to Scrambler. This game is about swapping and scrambling letters to make new words and fill the puzzle.

Your goal is to use as few guesses as possible to solve the puzzle. You can make guesses one row at a time, or try to solve multiple rows with one guess.

Swapping

Every word will use the same letters as the previous word, except for 1 letter that will be swapped.

Select the empty square below. Now swap out the 'W' in WASTE with another letter to create a new word

When you make a guess, correct words will be coloured green, and incorrect words will be coloured orange.

You should know, some puzzles have more than one way to solve them, so keep your mind open.

And if you're ever feeling overwhelmed, just remember: 4 of the letters you need are already in the previous word. And there's no harm in solving the puzzle one word at a time.
