SCRAMBLER

HOW TO PLAY
Solve the puzzle by entering letters to complete the hidden words in as few guesses as possible. To make it easier, you can make a guess with an only partially complete puzzle.

Between any word and the next one, 1 letter will always be swapped out for a different one. However, the order of letters may be rearranged.
